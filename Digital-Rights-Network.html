Our Philosophy

The Manifesto

Digital rights are fundamental human rights applied to the digital world.

Core Definition

We ensure people can use the internet, devices, and media safely, equally, and freely, protecting them from censorship, surveillance, and discrimination online. They cover everything from online access and data protection to freedom of speech.

Access

Bridging the digital divide.

Security

Protection from online harm.

Equality

Non-discrimination online.

Privacy & Data Protection

Safeguarding personal data from misuse by companies or governments. Your data is not their product.

Freedom of Expression

The right to express ideas and opinions online without censorship. The internet square must be open.
