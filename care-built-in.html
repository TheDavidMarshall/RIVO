Care, Built In.

It usually starts the same way.

  • Too many messages.
  • Codes that never arrive.
  • Accounts you don’t remember creating.
  • A feeling that your phone and inbox no longer belong to you.

For a lot of people, that’s just “how the internet is.”

We don’t think it has to be.

We met students who couldn’t afford another subscription. Small business owners drowning in spam. People locked out of accounts because their number changed. Communities where privacy tools exist — but feel out of reach.

They weren’t asking for more features. They were asking for quiet. For control. For something that just works.

So we built it differently.

We built a place where your digital life feels calmer. Where spam doesn’t pile up. Where you can finally see what’s connected to you — and let go of what isn’t.

And we built sign-ins that don’t depend on fragile text messages, so your access doesn’t disappear when your number changes or when someone tries to take it from you.

Care, built in — not added later.

Then we asked ourselves a harder question.

What about the people who need this most, but can’t afford another premium plan?

So this campaign is different.

We’re opening access. Partnering with universities in communities that need it most. Giving students and families a safer digital start — free.

Not a trial. Not a discount. A real chance at a quieter, more peaceful digital life. Because protection shouldn’t be a privilege.

If you’ve ever felt overwhelmed by the noise,
if you’ve ever helped someone fix a hacked account,
if you’ve ever wished the internet felt a little more human —

This is for you.

And for the people you care about.
