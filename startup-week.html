The Global Innovation Tour

7 Days. 7 Markets. Limitless Possibilities.
A movement connecting founders, investors, and global markets.

The Spark

Every great company begins with a spark — an idea, a connection, a moment where opportunity meets momentum. Startups Week was born from a simple belief: innovation shouldn’t be isolated. It should travel, collide, cross borders, and transform industries.

In a world where technology evolves daily, the next breakthrough can come from anywhere — a garage in California, a skyscraper in Dubai, a co-working loft in Paris, or a university lab in New Delhi.

“What if startups had one week where the entire world opened its doors to them?”

Not just a conference. Not just an expo. But a global innovation tour. A platform where founders, VCs, angel investors, corporates, SMEs, and tech enthusiasts can collide. Startups Week exists to change that.

If you’re building, funding, scaling, or exploring — this is your week.

The Vision

Startups Week is more than an event. It's a movement built on three pillars:

1
Connection
We bring together those who build, those who fund, and those who adopt new technology. Bridging gaps between siloed ecosystems.
2
Acceleration
We create environments where deals happen fast — because innovation moves fast. A curated space for partnerships and breakthroughs.
3
Access
We remove borders. We level playing fields. Whether you're a first-time founder or a unicorn in the making, you deserve global reach.
